Nagz

AI-Powered Family Reminders

Nagz is a family reminder app that helps parents and kids stay on top of tasks, chores, and responsibilities. Smart escalation handles the follow-ups so you don't have to repeat yourself.

Create "nags" for family members, track progress with gamification, and let AI mediation keep communication constructive. Supports guardian, participant, and child roles with COPPA-compliant safeguards.

Talk to Your Tasks

Just type what you need: "Remind me to call the dentist tomorrow" or "What's overdue?" The AI chat creates, completes, reschedules, and manages your nags — all conversationally. Choose from 6 AI personalities including Gordon Ramsay, Mr. Rogers, and a motivational coach.

On-Device AI — Private by Design

Nagz uses Apple Foundation Models to run AI features entirely on your iPhone — conversational chat, excuse summaries, tone selection, coaching tips, weekly digests, push-back reminders, task summaries, and gamification nudges. Pattern detection and completion prediction use fast local math.

Your text never leaves your device. Only structured data like categories and counts syncs to the server. No external AI services are used.
